**Table 1: Logic Symbols**

  **Symbol**   **Logical Operation**
  ------------ ----------------------------
  →            implication (if…then)
  ↔            equality (if and only if)
  ¬            negation (not)
  ∧            conjunction (and)
  ∨            inclusive disjunction (or)
